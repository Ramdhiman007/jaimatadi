Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about computer maintenance and our website.

Is this service free?
Yes! All the guides on Technical Help Hub are free to access. We believe in making technical support accessible to everyone.
How can I submit a new problem?
Navigate to the Submit Problem page. You'll need to fill out a form with the problem details and your solution. Our team reviews all submissions before publishing.
Do I need to create an account?
No, you can view all guides without an account. However, creating an account allows you to save your progress, bookmark issues, and track your submissions.
Is the data I enter safe?
This is a demo website. All "user data" (logins, submissions) is stored purely in your browser's LocalStorage and is never sent to any server. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser cache.
Can I request a specific guide?
Absolutely. If you can't find a solution, use the Contact Us form to request a guide, and we'll do our best to add it.
Why is my internet still slow after trying the guide?
Speed issues can be complex. If the software steps didn't help, it might be a hardware limitation of your router, interference from neighbors, or an ISP issue. We recommend contacting your service provider if the guide doesn't resolve it.
